INTERRELATIONSHIP BETWEEN FORMS

Ink on Fabriano 12x12".

This is an assignment for a 2-D course, where the guide is Wucius Wong's Elements of Design. For this excercise, the goal was to explore how forms relate to each other by experimenting with 4 planes and 3 lines.
